Privacy Policy for Quick-Screenshot

Effective Date: 20 June 2025

1. Data Collection

Quick-Screenshot does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data. All screenshot functionality is executed locally in your browser.

2. Use of Permissions

The extension uses minimal permissions solely to perform its screenshot features:

  • activeTab – to capture the visible content of the current browser tab.
  • scripting – to inject content scripts for selection functionality.
  • tabs and storage – for managing temporary screenshot data (only on your device).

3. No Third-Party Data Sharing

We do not send data to any external servers, nor do we share any data with third parties.

4. Security

All operations are client-side and confined to your local device. We never read, track, or transmit your browsing data or screenshots beyond what is necessary to display them in your browser.

5. Changes to This Policy

This privacy policy may be updated in the future. Changes will be posted on this page.
